# The 1000 Company Proposal

## Objective

Build a list of 1000 DT ICP-qualified manufacturing companies within 30 days using AI-assisted research, automation, and structured quality control.

---

# Assumptions

Target Profile:

* Revenue: ₹30 Cr – ₹500 Cr
* Founder-led or promoter-led
* Manufacturing companies
* Evidence of growth
* Operational systems maturity
* Leadership continuity

---

# Stage 1: Build the Initial Universe

4. LinkedIn
5. ACMA

7. CII
8. Udyam Database
9. Export Databases

Expected Output:

10,000+ companies

# Stage 2: Automated Qualification

AI agents collect:

* Revenue
* Industry
* Leadership
* Manufacturing evidence
* Certifications
* Export indicators

Automatic E1 Check:

Manufacturer?
Yes / No

Automatic E2 Check:

Indian operations?
Yes / No

Expected Yield:

10,000 → 4,000

# Stage 3: Federer Pre-Scoring

AI assigns provisional scores:

* C3
* C4
* C5
* C6

* C8

Expected Yield:

4,000 → 1,500

---

# Stage 4: Human Review

Analyst reviews:

* Top scoring companies
* Borderline cases
* Missing evidence

Reject:

* Large corporations
* PE-controlled firms
* Traders
* Commodity suppliers

Expected Yield:

1,500 → 1,000

---

# Quality Control Process

## QC Layer 1

Automated checks

* Revenue validation
* Industry validation
* Director validation

---

## QC Layer 2

Evidence review

Each score must have:

* Source
* Evidence
* Verification

---

## QC Layer 3

Random sampling

Review:

10% of final list

Measure:

* False positives
* Missing data
* Incorrect classifications
